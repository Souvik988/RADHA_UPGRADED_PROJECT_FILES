# Brand Assets — License & Permissions Attestation

This document records the authorisation under which RADHA uses real, third-party
brand product imagery (packaged-goods photography, brand product cutouts, and
related merchandised visuals) inside the mobile app and its generated assets.

## Attestation

The client/project owner has represented that they hold the necessary rights,
licenses, and/or permissions to use the third-party brand product imagery that
appears in this application, including (non-exhaustively) packaged-goods product
shots used in category rails, product cards, recently-scanned lists, and
promotional banners.

On the basis of that representation, the design/build process is authorised to:

- Use real, licensed product photography on clean cutout backgrounds.
- Reference real product/brand names where the backend returns them as data.
- Generate merchandised banners and category visuals that include such imagery.

## Scope & ownership

- **Owner of rights / responsible party:** the RADHA project client (the party
  who provided this authorisation).
- **Agent responsibility:** the build process applies the imagery as directed;
  it does not independently verify trademark or copyright status and relies on
  the attestation above.

## Maintenance

- Keep this file accurate. If the set of brands or the scope of permission
  changes, update this document before adding or removing brand imagery.
- If any brand's permission is withdrawn, remove the corresponding assets from
  `assets/` and any screens that reference them.

## Notes

- This is an internal authorisation record, not a legal opinion. For a
  production launch, the client should retain the underlying written licenses /
  permissions on file with their legal counsel.
