# Product

## Register

product

## Users

RADHA serves Indian retail operators, store-floor staff, auditors, managers, and consumer users who need fast product understanding. Business users work in noisy store-floor conditions: scanning items, checking expiry, reconciling GRN, tracking tasks, watching inventory health, and producing reports. Consumer users scan packaged goods, compare health signals, save products, manage shopping lists, and unlock premium product intelligence.

## Product Purpose

RADHA is a mobile-first retail audit and product intelligence platform. It helps users scan products, understand health and ingredient signals, detect expiry risk, verify EANs, manage lightweight inventory, create tasks, handle GRN flows, and view operational reports. It is not GST, POS, ERP, or accounting software. Success means the app feels trustworthy on the retail floor: fast, honest, localised, resilient offline, and clear when data is unavailable.

## Brand Personality

Calm, premium, practical, culturally rooted, and trustworthy. The visual language should feel Indian without becoming decorative or festive by default. Mor, the peacock companion, is a confident helper: expressive and memorable, but never childish.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic Material defaults, childish mascot art, fake progress, fake health claims, neon or purple-blue AI gradients, over-decorated cards, spreadsheet clutter, and POS/ERP/accounting visual metaphors. Do not make RADHA look like a toy app, a tax app, a supermarket checkout system, or a generic SaaS dashboard.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the floor worker in flow: surfaces should scan quickly, preserve touch target size, and avoid needless choreography.
2. Show operational truth: loading, empty, unavailable, offline, pending, and locked states must be honest.
3. Let Mor guide without distracting: use Mor to clarify mood and state, not to decorate every surface.
4. Preserve restrained Indian warmth: use the orange brand accent, deep ink, warm paper, and limited teal with discipline.
5. Build for continuation: assets, copy, and UI states must line up with the typed registry and localisation model.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, 44 pt minimum touch targets, text scale resilience, reduced-motion behavior, semantic labels for meaningful images, and non-color cues for status. The app supports English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, and Marathi; Gujarati is not part of the supported locale set.
